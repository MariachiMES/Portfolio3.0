about.Me


My name is David Ortiz. I am an artist, musician, creator and web developer.

My great passion in life has been creativity, and mostly prolifically: my music. My most recent endeavors being Mariachi Entertainment System and Studio Comal, creative outlets for my musicianship and editing skills. I have taken on the task of learning web development with the hopes of building a career that will bring me personal satisfaction, a sense of stabiliy and the ability to bring my creative drive to a new medium. I am currently enrolled at UTSA full-stack bootcamp.

My first opportunity to work in the games industry came when I received a commission from Riot Games to create a mariachi version of an existing League of Legends song "The Curse of The Sad Mummy". I was able to continue my work in the games industry by working on original music for DrinkBox Studios' Guacamelee! 2 as well as working on mariachi covers of existing music from the franchise also featured in-game.

My most recent work on games is on an independent steam game called "Town of Zoz", and action-adventure RPG about a boy and his connection to his indigenous culture through food.
